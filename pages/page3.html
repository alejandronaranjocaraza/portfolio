Ground Beef Tacos

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 packet taco seasoning mix
  • 3/4 cup water
  • 8 taco shells
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1/2 cup chopped tomatoes
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. In a skillet over medium heat, cook ground beef until browned. Drain fat.
  2. Add taco seasoning and water to skillet. Simmer for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. Spoon beef into taco shells and top with lettuce, tomatoes, and cheese. Serve immediately.
