Classic Mac and Cheese

Ingredients

  • 2 cups uncooked elbow macaroni
  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • 4 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper

Steps

  1. Cook macaroni according to package directions; drain and set aside.
  2. In a saucepan, melt butter and whisk in flour until smooth. Gradually add milk, stirring constantly until thickened.
  3. Add cheese, salt, and pepper. Stir until cheese melts. Combine with macaroni and serve hot.
